# How to Add APASS to Your Wallet (Simple Guide)

You can store APASS tokens in any **BEP-20 compatible wallet** such as **MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or Binance Web3 Wallet**.

Follow these simple steps:

---

## Step 1: Install a Wallet (if you don’t have one)

Download one of these apps on your phone:

- MetaMask  
- Trust Wallet  
- Binance Web3 Wallet (inside the Binance app)

Create your wallet and **save your recovery phrase safely** (this is very important).

---

## Step 2: Make Sure You Are on BNB Smart Chain (BSC)     

APASS runs on **BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)**.

In your wallet:
- Switch network to **BNB Smart Chain**

(Most wallets like Trust Wallet and Binance Web3 Wallet already support it by default.)

---

## Step 3: Add APASS Token

You need to add APASS so it appears in your wallet.

### APASS Contract Address    

`0x8f5c7779D860558AD236dC45C247c55b5D7BB3D5`

---

### In MetaMask

1. Tap + to **Import Tokens**  
2. Select **Custom**
3. Paste the APASS contract address  
4. Token Name: Africa Tourism Pass   
5. Symbol: APASS    
6. Decimals: 18
7. Tap **Import**

---

### In Trust Wallet

1. Tap the **top-right filter icon**  
2. Scroll down and tap **Add Custom Token**  
3. Select **BNB Smart Chain**  
4. Paste the contract address  
5. Tap **Save**

---

### In Binance Web3 Wallet

1. Open the **Binance App → Web3 Wallet**  
2. Tap **Add Tokens**  
3. Paste the contract address  
4. Confirm to add APASS  

---

### Note

- Your APASS balance may show as **0** until tokens are sent to your wallet  
- The token logo may not appear immediately in some wallets  

---

You will now see **APASS** in your wallet.

---

## Step 4: Receive APASS Tokens

To receive tokens:

1. Copy your wallet address  
2. Share it with the sender  

Once sent, your APASS tokens will appear in your wallet.

---

### What is a Wallet Address?

Your wallet address is like your **account number**. It is a long string of letters and numbers.

You can safely share your wallet address to receive APASS tokens, but **never share your recovery phrase**.

---

## Step 5: You’re Ready to Use APASS

You can now:

- Hold APASS in your wallet  
- Transfer APASS to others  
- Use APASS at participating tourism providers  
- Earn rewards and points through travel activity  

---

## Important Tips

- Never share your **recovery phrase** with anyone  
- Only use the official contract address above  
- Always use the **BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)** network when receiving APASS  
- You may need a small amount of **BNB** in your wallet to send or transfer tokens  
- Double-check everything before sending or receiving tokens
